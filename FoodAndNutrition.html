Food & Nutrition

Course Information

Curriculum Leader: Miss C Lobo

Why study Design and Technology – Home Economics Food and Nutrition?

Studying this course will encourage you to:-

  • Extend and apply your skills knowledge and understanding of food and nutrition in a variety of situations.
  • Develop your knowledge and understanding of human needs within a diverse society.
  • Increase your knowledge and understanding of relevant technological and scientific developments within the food industry.

What will I study?

  • Nutrient, diet and health issues throughout life.
  • Factors affecting consumer choice.
  • Nutritional, physical, chemical and sensory properties of foods in storage, preparation and cooking.
  • Food hygiene and safety.

How will I be assessed?

  • Principles of Food and Nutrition – examination 2 hours (40%)
  • Food and nutrition Practical Task – controlled assessment (60%)
