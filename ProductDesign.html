Product Design

Course Information

Curriculum Leader: Miss C Lobo

Why study Design and Technology – Product Design?

Studying this course will encourage you to:-

  • Design and make products with creativity using a range of materials and techniques. The advantage of this course is that you can study and make products with paper/card, timber, metals, plastics, electronics, etc, depending on your choice.
  • Become more aware of sustainability and the environmental impact of your designing.

What will I study?

  • Materials and components – classification and working properties of a range of materials. As a minimum, you will study paper/card, and one other material area.
  • Design and market influences, product development, how products change over time and how to communicate your ideas.
  • Product marketing, human factors, safety, ethical, environmental and sustainability issues.

How will I be assessed?

  • Written examination – 2 hours (40%)
  • Design folder and practical project – controlled assessment (60%)
