Textiles Technology

Course Information

Curriculum Leader: Miss C Lobo

Why study Design and Technology – Textiles Technology?

Studying this course will encourage you to:-

  • Design and make items which can enhance your home environments – personal and home accessories, soft furnishings and garments. Sustainability needs to be considered during the design and make process.
  • Study the fabrics you will be using, looking at their origins as well as their properties, whether as pure or as mixed fabrics and how these properties affect their end use.
  • Use computer controlled sewing machines and state of the art CAD/CAM for image transfers on to your products.

What will I study?

  • How to be creative when designing.
  • How to design and make products to meet the needs of clients and consumers.
  • Fibres and fabrics, their properties and characteristics
  • Finishing processes – dyeing and printing, decoration and enhancement. These are just a few things; there are many other areas of study.

How will I be assessed?

  • Written examination – 2 hours (40%)
  • Design folder and practical project – controlled assessment (60%)
